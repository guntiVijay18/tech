Alumni

Alumni are former students or graduates of an institution who continue to be connected to their school, college, or university even after completing their studies. They play a valuable role in strengthening the reputation of the institution by sharing their achievements, career journeys, and experiences. Alumni networks often provide mentorship, guidance, and professional opportunities to current students, helping them build connections in the industry. They also contribute by supporting events, placements, and sometimes even offering financial or resource support to their alma mater. Strong alumni relations create a bridge between past and present students, fostering a sense of community and pride.
