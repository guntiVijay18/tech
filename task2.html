About Us

Tech Jobs is a non-profit organization established in 1996 by Dr Apj Abdul Kalam, focused on empowering low-income communities in India through improved education, health, and livelihood outcomes, with a particular emphasis on climate action and inclusion, especially for people with disabilities and women. The foundation develops and tests innovative solutions to address social problems, leverages partnerships to scale its impact, and aligns its work with the UN Sustainable Development Goals to create lasting, sustainable change.
